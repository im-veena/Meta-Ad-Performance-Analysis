# 📊 Meta Ad Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Advertising Performance Analysis Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**.

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze digital marketing campaign performance, understand user engagement behavior, and provide actionable insights to improve ROI (Return on Investment).

It transforms raw advertising event data into meaningful business insights across **reach, engagement, conversions, audience behavior, and budget efficiency**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement

Digital marketing teams invest significant budgets into online advertisements but often lack clarity on:

- Which campaigns are performing effectively
- Which audience segments generate the highest engagement
- Which ad formats deliver better conversions
- How user behavior changes across time and geography
- How to optimize budget allocation for maximum ROI

This dashboard is designed to solve these challenges through data-driven insights.

---

## 🧠 Key Objectives

- Analyze ad performance across multiple platforms (Facebook & Instagram)
- Track user engagement behavior across demographics
- Measure conversion efficiency through funnel metrics
- Identify high-performing ad formats and campaigns
- Provide optimization strategies for marketing teams

## 📂 Dataset & Data Model

The project is built on a **Star Schema Architecture** for efficient reporting.

### 📌 Fact Table
- Ad Events (Impressions, Clicks, Purchases, Engagements)

### 📌 Dimension Tables
- Ads (Ad type, platform, targeting details)
- Campaigns (Budget, duration, campaign details)
- Users (Demographics, location, interests)

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks and evaluates:

- Total Impressions
- Clicks
- Shares & Comments
- Purchases (Conversions)
- Engagement Rate
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Conversion Rate
- Purchase Rate
- Campaign Budget Utilization

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Executive KPI Overview
Provides a high-level summary of campaign performance including reach, engagement, and conversions.

### 2. Audience Analysis
- Gender-based engagement distribution
- Age group performance (18–30, 30–45, etc.)
- Target vs actual audience behavior

### 3. Geographic Analysis
- Country-wise engagement distribution
- High-performing regions identification

### 4. Time-Based Analysis
- Hourly engagement trends
- Weekly performance trends
- Calendar heatmap for campaign activity

### 5. Ad Performance Analysis
- Comparison of Ad Types (Video, Image, Carousel, Stories)
- CTR and Conversion comparison
- Funnel performance analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Audience Insights
- Female users showed higher engagement compared to male users
- Age group 18–30 represents the most active audience segment

### 📌 Geographic Insights
- India and the United States are the top-performing regions
- Certain regions show higher conversion potential than others

### 📌 Ad Performance Insights
- Video ads outperform other ad formats in CTR and engagement
- Story ads also show strong reach and interaction

### 📌 Behavioral Insights
- Engagement peaks during afternoon and evening hours
- Specific campaign periods show spikes due to promotions

### 📌 Conversion Insights
- High engagement does not always translate into purchases
- Funnel drop-off indicates optimization opportunity in landing pages

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing ad formats (Video & Stories)
- Focus targeting on high-engagement demographics (18–30 age group)
- Optimize landing pages to improve conversion rates
- Schedule campaigns during peak engagement hours (Afternoon & Evening)
- Improve retargeting strategies to reduce funnel drop-offs
- Reallocate budget toward high-performing geographies
